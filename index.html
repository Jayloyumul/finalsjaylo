About Wave Cafe

Wave Cafe, a cozy and modern coffee shop, was founded by Jaylo Yumul, a 20-year-old IT student with a passion for both technology and coffee. The café has quickly become a popular spot for students and professionals alike, offering a variety of handcrafted beverages and pastries in a welcoming atmosphere.

Wave Cafe is known for its innovative approach to coffee, blending traditional brewing methods with cutting-edge technology to create a unique and memorable customer experience.

How we began

Wave Café began as a vision of Jaylo Yumul, a 21-year-old IT student who wanted to merge his love for technology with his passion for coffee. Inspired by the idea of creating a space where students and professionals could gather, Jaylo meticulously planned the café's concept, combining traditional coffee brewing with modern technological advancements.

With limited initial funds, Jaylo turned to crowdfunding and community support to bring his dream to life. After months of hard work, renovations, and fine-tuning the perfect menu, Wave Café opened its doors and quickly became a local favorite, celebrated for its innovative approach and welcoming atmosphere.
